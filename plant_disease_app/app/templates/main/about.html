About PlantDoc

Final Year Project - AI-Powered Plant Disease Detection System

Project Overview

PlantDoc is an AI-powered web application designed to help farmers, gardeners, and agricultural professionals identify plant diseases quickly and accurately.

Using state-of-the-art deep learning techniques, specifically Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN), our system can analyze images of plant leaves and identify diseases with high accuracy, providing treatment recommendations and prevention tips.

Training Dataset

Our model is trained on the PlantVillage dataset, one of the largest publicly available datasets for plant disease classification.

  • Over 50,000 labeled images
  • 38 disease classes across 14 species
  • Originally published by Sporran et al.
  • Available on Kaggle and HuggingFace
