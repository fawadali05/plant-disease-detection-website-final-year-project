How It Works

Get started with plant disease detection in four simple steps

1

Upload Your Image

Take a clear photo of a plant leaf showing symptoms, or upload an existing image from your gallery. For best results, ensure good lighting and focus on the affected area.

  • Supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF
  • Maximum file size: 16MB
  • Ensure the leaf with symptoms is clearly visible
2

AI Analysis

Our deep learning model, trained on the PlantVillage dataset with over 50,000 images, analyzes your image to identify disease patterns and classify the condition.

  • Powered by CNN (ResNet50/MobileNetV2)
  • 38 disease classes across 14 plant species
  • Processing takes just a few seconds
3

Get Diagnosis

Receive instant diagnosis with the disease name, affected plant type, and a confidence score indicating how certain the AI is about the prediction.

  • Detailed disease information
  • Category classification (fungal, bacterial, viral)
  • Multiple prediction options shown
4

Treatment Recommendations

Get comprehensive treatment plans including organic and chemical options, prevention tips, and recommended pesticides for effective recovery.

  • Step-by-step treatment guide
  • Prevention and recovery tips
  • Downloadable PDF report
